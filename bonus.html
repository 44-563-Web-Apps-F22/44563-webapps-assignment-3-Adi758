Panda

Panda also known as the panda bear or simply the panda, is a bear species endemic to China.It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The name panda is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda, a neighboring musteloid. Though it belongs to the order Carnivora, the giant panda is a folivore, with bamboo shoots and leaves making up more than 99% of its diet.

  1. A 2007 report showed 239 pandas living in captivity inside China and another 27 outside the country. By December 2014, 49 pandas lived in captivity outside China, living in 18 zoos in 13 countries.
  2. Some reports also show that the number of pandas in the wild is on the rise.
  3. By March 2015, the wild panda population had increased to 1,864 individuals.
